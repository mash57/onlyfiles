Strategic positioning

Do not compete with mass trekking companies. Own the space between trekking and real alpinism.
Core wedge

Trekkers who want to become climbers

Urban runners, serious trekkers, CrossFit types, founders and professionals who want actual mountain skills, not only summit photos.

Product promise

Skill + objective

Every trip should answer one question: “What will I learn?” or “What peak will I climb?” Mixing both loosely makes the product fuzzy.

Brand moat

Ratios signal safety

Use 4:1 for beginner training, 3:1 for alpine skills, 2:1 for progression, and 1:1 for premium summit/private guiding.

Product ladder

The main business logic: acquire through accessible weekends, convert to skills, then progress to peaks and private guiding.
Acquire

Weekend Rock Skills

Low-friction product for first-time climbers. Aim: build trust, qualify serious clients, upsell to Alpine Skills.

Convert

Alpine Skills Manali

The signature course. Snow craft, crampons, rope systems, anchors, self-arrest, basic rescue and mountain movement.

Grow

Friendship → Deo Tibba → Hanuman Tibba

Clear progression from introductory summit to 6,000m expedition to serious technical Himachal objective.

Operating model

The business needs high trust, not high volume. Bad guide ratios will kill the premium positioning.

Core team

1 Lead IFMGA/NIM-grade head guide as face of brand; 3–4 empanelled lead guides; 6–8 assistant guides; cook/helper pool; one ops and sales coordinator in Manali.

Guide ratios

Weekend rock: 4:1. Alpine Skills: 3:1. Friendship: 3:1, tightening on summit day. Deo Tibba: 3:1 or 2:1. Hanuman Tibba: 2:1 or 1:1 for key sections.

Included services

Guide, assistant guide, technical group gear, tents, food on mountain, local transport, permits assistance, safety briefing and pre-trip fitness checklist.

Excluded services

Personal clothing, boots, insurance, travel to Manali, hotel stay before/after trip, evacuation cost, personal porter unless sold as add-on.

Year-one target

Keep the business intentionally small and premium. The goal is credibility, repeat clients and clean operations — not noisy volume.

Sales and marketing priorities

SEO

Own Manali climbing intent

Create serious pages for “Friendship Peak guided climb”, “Alpine skills course India”, “Deo Tibba expedition” and “private mountain guide Manali”.

Community

Target climber-adjacent groups

Runners, trekkers, gyms, CrossFit boxes, mountaineering alumni, outdoor clubs, founders and senior professionals.

Trust

Sell safety visibly

Publish ratios, guide credentials, rescue plan, weather decision rules, gear list, fitness requirements and honest difficulty ratings.
